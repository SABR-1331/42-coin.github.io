SABR 25 by 2025

SABR ambition is to have 25 Nation States take advantage of SABR by creating a bespoke national blockchain currency with all of the SABR attributes. Each Nation will have its fully enabled digital blockchain currency leveling the playing field and allowing fully transparent, low cost, safe enabled transactions. Blockchain’s unique technology was created for and used by people in areas of the world with limited financial infrastructures, the unbanked and unbankable. Now, ALL can have full global access to financial capacities. SABR provides a digital blockchain currency for the citizens of the Nation States, and provides users with immediate access and executions on all types of personal, retail, commercial and industrial financial transactions.
